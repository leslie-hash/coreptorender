## Taking Action

### Option 1: Approve
1. Add optional notes (coverage plan, special instructions)
2. Click **"Approve"** button
3. Request moves to next stage (client approval or payroll)

### Option 2: Deny
1. **Required:** Add notes explaining why
2. Click **"Deny"** button
3. Team member is notified with your explanation

### Your Notes Matter!
- Help payroll understand the situation
- Document special circumstances
- Provide context for client approval
- Create clear audit trail

---

## After You Approve

### What Happens Next:

**1. Client Approval (if required)**
- Request forwarded to client
- Client reviews and approves/denies
- You're notified of client decision

**2. Payroll Notification**
- Approved requests sent to payroll automatically
- Synced with Belina Payroll system
- Updates Google Sheets records

**3. System Updates**
- Team member's PTO balance updated
- Attendance records adjusted
- Calendar marked
- All stakeholders notified

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Insufficient PTO Balance
**What to do:**
1. Deny the request
2. Note: "Insufficient PTO balance"
3. Suggest: "You have X days available, consider shorter duration"

### Scenario 2: Coverage Concerns
**What to do:**
1. Add note: "Need coverage plan"
2. Contact team member to coordinate
3. Approve once coverage confirmed

### Scenario 3: Last-Minute Request
**What to do:**
1. Check if it's emergency (sick leave)
2. If emergency: Approve quickly
3. If planned: Note the late submission, approve if possible

### Scenario 4: Team Member on Email
**What to do:**
1. Can still email you (some team members prefer this)
2. You enter the request on their behalf in LeavePoint
3. Or ask them to submit via LeavePoint for tracking

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: "No requests showing"
**Solutions:**
- Verify your email in system matches your login
- Check if team members are assigned to you
- Refresh the page
- Contact admin if issue persists

### Issue: "Can't approve request"
**Solutions:**
- Check team member's PTO balance
- Verify dates are in future
- Ensure no system validation errors
- Try logging out and back in

### Issue: "Notification not received"
**Solutions:**
- Check notification settings
- Verify email address
- Check spam folder
- Enable in-app notifications
