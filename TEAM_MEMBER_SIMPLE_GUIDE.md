## â“ Common Questions

### **Q: How do I know my request was received?**
A: You get instant confirmation with a request ID (e.g., REQ-2025-357). Check "Notifications" to see all updates.

### **Q: Can I see my PTO balance?**
A: Yes! When you open the "Submit Leave Request" form, your current PTO balance is displayed at the top.

### **Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Average 1-2 hours (CSP review + Client approval). You'll get notifications at each step.

### **Q: Can I cancel a request?**
A: Contact your CSP directly. They can modify or cancel pending requests.

### **Q: What if I need emergency leave?**
A: Submit the request and mark it as urgent. Notify your CSP immediately via phone/email as well.

### **Q: Can I see past requests?**
A: Not in the team member view. Contact your CSP if you need historical data. Your focus is submitting new requests.

### **Q: What happens after approval?**
A: Payroll automatically receives the document. Your PTO balance updates. You get a notification confirming everything is complete.
