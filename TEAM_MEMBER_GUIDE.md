# Team Member Guide - Submitting Leave Requests

## How to Submit a Leave Request

### Step 1: Log into LeavePoint
1. Open LeavePoint: http://localhost:8080 (or your company URL)
2. Log in with your @zimworx.com email

### Step 2: Navigate to Leave Request Form
1. Click **"Leave Requests"** in the sidebar
2. Or click **"Submit New Request"** button

### Step 3: Fill Out the Form
**Required Information:**
- **Team Member Name**: Your full name (auto-filled if logged in)
- **Leave Type**: Select from dropdown
  - Annual Leave
  - Sick Leave
  - Personal Leave
  - Maternity/Paternity Leave
- **Start Date**: First day of leave
- **End Date**: Last day of leave
- **Reason**: Brief explanation for your leave

**Optional:**
- Additional notes or special circumstances

### Step 4: Submit
1. Click **"Submit Leave Request"** button
2. You'll see a confirmation message
3. Your request is automatically assigned to your CSP

## Common Questions

**Q: Who is my CSP?**
A: Your CSP is automatically assigned based on your client/team. The system knows your CSP, so you don't need to look it up.

**Q: Can I submit multiple requests?**
A: Yes! You can submit as many requests as you have PTO days for.

**Q: What if I need to cancel a request?**
A: Contact your CSP directly. They can cancel or modify pending requests.

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Usually 1-3 business days, depending on your CSP's workload and client approval requirements.

**Q: Can I still email my CSP instead?**
A: You can, but using LeavePoint is faster and ensures proper tracking. Your CSP may ask you to submit via LeavePoint anyway.

**Q: What if I'm out sick and can't access LeavePoint?**
A: Email your CSP. They can enter the request on your behalf.

**Q: Do I need to upload any forms?**
A: Not required! The system generates all necessary forms automatically. But you can upload a form if you prefer.
